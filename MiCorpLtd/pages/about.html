About Micorp Trading LLC

Your Trusted Trading Partner Since 2010

Our Story

Founded in 2010, Micorp Trading LLC has grown from a small trading company to a premier general trading company in Dubai, specializing in Electronics, IT Products, and Automobiles.

With our headquarters in the heart of Dubai, we've built a reputation for reliability, quality, and exceptional customer service across the UAE and beyond.

Our Mission

To bridge innovation and reliability by providing high-quality products and exceptional service to our clients, while maintaining the highest standards of business ethics and fostering sustainable growth.

Our Vision

To become the most trusted and innovative trading partner in the region, known for our integrity, comprehensive product portfolio, and unwavering customer-centric approach.
